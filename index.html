Maplewood Community Garden

Contructed by a group of residents with a common vision of therapuetic gardening to create downstream healthy benefits for the young and old. Inclusive of community activities such as Harvesting Day and Educational Open Houses, the garden allows for interaction within the community and a solace from our stressful surroundings.

Mutual Learning

Learn and share about new herbs and spices and even exchange culinary recipies for cooking. Gain knowledge about business principles and working as a team by participating in the food supply chain.

Therapuetic Gardening

Promotes mindfulness and connection with nature. Encourages low intensity exercise and improve motor skills for the elderly while fostering a sense of accountability and achievement within children.

Build Friendships

Draw neighbours together to meet, get their hands dirty and form friendships across generations and cultural divides.
